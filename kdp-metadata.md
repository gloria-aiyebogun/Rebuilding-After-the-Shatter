# Amazon KDP Publishing Guide — Rebuilding after the Shatter

Use this file when setting up the book on kdp.amazon.com.

---

## Step 1 — Create Your KDP Account

Go to **https://kdp.amazon.com** and sign in with an existing Amazon account or create a new one. Use Gloria's details since she is the author and rights holder.

### Tax information (W-8BEN form)
Amazon requires this even for non-US authors. It confirms you are not a US taxpayer and affects how much withholding tax is applied to royalties. Fill it in honestly — it is straightforward.

### Bank / Payment details
KDP pays royalties by bank transfer. Nigerian bank accounts can be used, but many African authors use **Wise** (wise.com) or **Payoneer** as they handle international transfers from Amazon more reliably. Set this up before publishing so royalties are not held.

---

## Step 2 — Set Up Amazon Author Central

Before or right after publishing, go to **https://authorcentral.amazon.com** and create Gloria's author page. This is where her:
- **Author photo** appears
- **Full bio** is displayed
- **All her books** are linked together under one profile

It is free and takes 15 minutes. Worth doing early so the page is live when the book goes on sale.

## Step 4 — Create the Print Paperback Listing

On KDP dashboard → **Create** → **Paperback**

Use the same title, subtitle, author, description, keywords and categories as above.

### Print settings

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Trim size | 6 × 9 inches |
| Interior type | Black & white |
| Paper colour | White (or cream — slightly warmer feel) |
| Cover finish | Matte (more premium feel than glossy) |
| Bleed | No bleed required |

### Upload files

- **Interior (manuscript):** `_book/Rebuilding-after-the-Shatter.pdf`
- **Cover:** Use **KDP Cover Creator** — upload `Cover.png` as the front cover, paste the back cover blurb below, and KDP builds the full wrap automatically

### Back cover blurb (for KDP Cover Creator)

Heartbreak doesn't just break your heart. It shakes your identity, silences your voice, and leaves you wondering if you will ever feel whole again.

*Rebuilding after the Shatter* is a compassionate, practical guide for every woman who has loved deeply and been left broken. Written by a psychologist with nearly a decade of counselling experience, this book walks with you through the full journey of healing — from understanding your pain, to releasing the past, to reclaiming your worth and opening your heart again.

With eight gentle chapters and a personal reflection exercise in each one, this is not a book you simply read. It is a companion you return to — on the hard days, the quiet days, and the days when you finally begin to feel like yourself again.

**You are not broken. You are becoming.**

### ISBN

- Amazon provides a free KDP ISBN for paperbacks — use it unless Gloria has her own
- Free ISBN means "Independently published" appears as the publisher name
- To show a custom publisher name, purchase a separate ISBN from your country's ISBN agency

### Pricing

- KDP print cost for 48 pages at 6×9: approximately **$2.15 per copy**
- Recommended list price: **$9.99–$12.99** (earns a reasonable royalty after print cost)
- Use KDP's royalty calculator to check the exact margin before publishing

## Step 5 — Submit for Review

KDP reviews submissions within **24–72 hours**. Once approved both listings go live on Amazon automatically. You will receive an email confirmation.

After approval, return to Amazon Author Central to link both listings (ebook and paperback) to Gloria's author profile.
