## Book Description (copy-paste into Amazon)

Have you been lying awake at night wondering if the pain will ever stop?

Heartbreak doesn't just break your heart — it shakes your entire world. It leaves you questioning your worth, your future, and everything you thought you knew about love. If you've ever felt completely shattered by the end of a relationship, <em>Rebuilding after the Shatter</em> was written for you.

In this compassionate and deeply practical guide, psychologist Gloria Aiyebogun walks with you through the full arc of healing — from the raw, aching pain of loss to the quiet, powerful freedom of a life rebuilt on your own terms. Drawing on psychological insight and genuine empathy, she offers not quick fixes, but honest companionship and real tools for the journey.

<strong>Inside these pages, you will:</strong>

<ul>
<li>Understand why heartbreak hurts so deeply — and why your pain is completely valid</li>
<li>Learn how to break free from the silence and suppression that keep you stuck</li>
<li>Release the past without losing yourself in the process</li>
<li>Heal the invisible wounds of rejection, betrayal, and self-blame</li>
<li>Rebuild your confidence and rediscover your worth from the inside out</li>
<li>Find wholeness again — on your own terms, at your own pace</li>
<li>Open your heart to love again, wisely and without fear</li>
<li>Live in genuine emotional freedom — calm, grounded, and fully yourself</li>
</ul>

Each chapter ends with a personal reflection exercise that gently guides you deeper into your own healing. This isn't just a book to read — it's a companion to return to again and again.

<strong>You are not broken. You are becoming.</strong>

<em>Rebuilding after the Shatter</em> is for every woman who has ever loved deeply and wondered if she would survive it. You will — and this book will walk every step with you.

## Pricing Guidance

KDP sets a minimum list price based on file size and royalty tier.

- **Kindle ebook:** $2.99–$9.99 earns 70% royalty. $9.99 is typical for self-help ebooks of this length. Consider $6.99 or $7.99 to stay competitive.
- **Paperback:** Use KDP's royalty calculator. At 6×9 with ~100 pages, a price of $12.99–$14.99 is reasonable and still earns a good margin.
